Who is Maya?

A Young Woman 'Discovered' by Technology

My name is Maya Parnell. My technological journey 'found' me during the Summer of 2017 in Silicon Valley, California. As I wandered through the Google X campus, passed Standford University and into the office of Bill Hewlett and Dave Packard (founders of HP Corporation), something inside told me, "I belong here".

Months after my visit to Silicon Valley, graduation from Michigan State University and beginning my first corporate position at Campbell-Ewald, I still thought about interest in tech. I didn't understand "why" I wanted to learn more about technology, but I continued to seek out information.

Then, I came across an opportunity to attend a Grand Circus workshop. I left my position as an Assistant Media Buyer and never looked back. I picked up literature about the fundamentals of computer language and programming, beginner's coding manuals and saw that I loved the learning process of coding.

And now...here I am!
